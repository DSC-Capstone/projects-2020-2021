About

For those who work out at home, finding a good workout routine is difficult. It is hard to constantly have to find workout videos that meet your fitness needs, as well as your time and equipment constraints. Plus, the pre-set workouts found online are not one-size-fits-all. In other words, while there are many workout videos online to choose from, these choices are non-personalized. Asnapp is a solution that can solve all these problems, as well as meet the growing need for an easy way to build a good at home workout routine during the pandemic.

Asnapp is a workout recommender system that recommends workout videos by Fitness Blender. Asnapp saves you the time and effort needed to build a good workout routine! It is as simple as:

  1. Register an account with us and tell us your available equipment and workout preferences. You can update these preferences anytime!
  2. Choose between our three recommendations models to generate your recommendations.
  3. Browse through our recommendation lists. We split up the recommendations by upper body, lower body, core, and total body workouts, and only the recommended workouts that match your available equipment and workout preferences are shown to you!
  4. Start a workout! Like or dislike a workout to get more personalized recommendations.

Asnapp was created by Amanda Shu, Najeem Kanishka, and Peter Peng for the UCSD DSC180A/B Capstone Project.
